## Cake Recipe

### Classic Chocolate Cake

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup hot water
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
2. Combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
3. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
4. Stir in hot water (batter will be thin).
5. Pour batter evenly into the prepared pans.
6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
7. Cool in pans for 10 minutes; remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
8. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

Makes one 2-layer cake (serves 12).
